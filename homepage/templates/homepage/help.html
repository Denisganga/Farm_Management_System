Employee Details Help

In the Employee Details section, you can manage information related to the employees working on your farm. This section includes details such as Employee ID, Name, Country Code, Phone Number, Position, Salary, and Performance.

Fields Explanation:

  • Employee ID: Unique identifier for each employee.
  • Name: Full name of the employee.
  • Country Code: Country code for the employee's phone number.
  • Phone Number: Contact number for the employee.
  • Position: Job role or position of the employee.
  • Salary: Compensation or wage for the employee.
  • Performance: Evaluation of the employee's performance.

Actions:

The "Actions" column provides options to interact with employee records:

  • Add: Click this button to add a new employee to the system.
